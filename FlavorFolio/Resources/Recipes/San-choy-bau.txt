Title
San-choy-bau
Introduction

Ingredients
1 tbsp--peanut oil
2--garlic cloves
2cm piece--ginger
500g--Coles Extra Lean Pork Mince
1 tbsp--soy sauce
2 tbsp--oyster sauce
3--shallotsthinly sliced
1/2--limejuiced
1 tsp--sesame oil
1 cup--beansprouts, trimmed
12 large--lettuce leaves
1/4 cup--fresh coriander leaves
1/4 cup--chopped roasted peanuts, optional

Directions
Heat wok over high heat until hot. Add peanut oil and swirl to coat. Add garlic, 
ginger and pork. Stir-fry for 2 to 3 minutes or until pork just changes colour
Add soy sauce and oyster. Add shallots, 2 teaspoons of lime juice and sesame oil 
to wok. Stir-fry for 3 minutes or until heated through. Stir in beansprouts.
Spoon pork mixture into lettuce leaves. Sprinkle with coriander and peanuts, if using. Serve.
