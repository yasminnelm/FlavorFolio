Ingredients
3cups--Romaine lettuce, chopped
1/2cup--Croutons
1/4cup--Parmesan cheese, shaved
1/4cup--Caesar dressing
1tsp--Anchovy paste (optional)
1clove--Garlic, minced
2tbsp--Lemon juice

Directions
In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce with croutons.
In a small bowl, whisk together the Caesar dressing, anchovy paste (if using), minced garlic, and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat the lettuce and croutons evenly.
Sprinkle shaved Parmesan cheese on top.
Serve the salad fresh and crisp.
Enjoy your refreshing and classic Caesar salad!
