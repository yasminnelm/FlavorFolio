Title
Impossible-quiche
Introduction

Ingredients
125g--ham
1--small onion
1 1/2 cups (180g)--  cheese
1/3 cup (50g)-- Coles white self-raising flour
4--eggs
1 1/2 cups(375ml)--milk

Directions
Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Grease a 5 cup capacity pie dish
Combine ham, onion, cheese, flour, salt and pepper in a medium bowl. Scatter over base of dish.
Whisk eggs and milk together in a large jug and pour over ham mixture. Cook for about 40 minutes 
or until puffed and golden. Cool slightly. Serve warm or cold.
