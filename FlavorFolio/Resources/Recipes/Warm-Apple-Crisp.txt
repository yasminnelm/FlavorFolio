Title
Warm-Apple-Crisp
Introduction

Ingredients
4cups--Apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
3/4cup--Brown sugar
1/2cup--Flour
1/2cup--Rolled oats
1/3cup--Butter, softened
3/4tsp--Ground cinnamon
3/4tsp--Ground nutmeg
Pinch of--Salt

Directions
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
Place the sliced apples in a baking dish.
In a bowl, mix together brown sugar, flour, oats, cinnamon, nutmeg, and a pinch of salt. Add softened butter and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Sprinkle the oat mixture evenly over the apples.
Bake in the preheated oven for about 45 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the apples are tender.
Serve the apple crisp warm, optionally with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.
Enjoy the comforting warmth of this delicious apple crisp!
