Title
Homemade-Chicken-Noodle-Soup
Introduction

Ingredients
2cups--Chicken, cooked and shredded
1cup--Carrots, diced
1cup--Celery, diced
1/2cup--Onion, chopped
2cloves--Garlic, minced
6cups--Chicken broth
1cup--Egg noodles
1tsp--Thyme
1tsp--Oregano
Salt and pepper, to taste
2tbsp--Olive oil

Directions
In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions, carrots, and celery. Cook until vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.
Add garlic, thyme, and oregano. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
Add the egg noodles to the pot and cook according to package instructions.
Add the cooked, shredded chicken to the pot. Season with salt and pepper. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
Serve the soup warm and garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
Enjoy your comforting bowl of homemade chicken noodle soup!
