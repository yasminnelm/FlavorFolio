Title
Simple Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce
Introduction

Ingredients
500g--Spaghetti
2cups--Tomato sauce
3cloves--Garlic, minced
1tbsp--Olive oil
1tsp--Salt
1/2tsp--Black pepper
1/4cup--Fresh basil, chopped
1/4cup--Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)

Directions
Cook spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
Add tomato sauce to the saucepan. Season with salt and pepper. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Toss the cooked spaghetti with the tomato sauce. Mix well until the pasta is evenly coated.
Garnish with chopped basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
  Serve hot and enjoy!
