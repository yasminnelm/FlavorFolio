Title
Classic-Grilled-Cheese-Sandwich
Introduction
Indulge in the timeless comfort of a Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich, where simplicity meets satisfaction. This quick and easy recipe brings together the perfect 
blend of crispy golden bread and gooey melted cheese, making it a go-to favorite for any occasion.

Ingredients
2slices--Bread
2slices--Cheddar cheese
2tbsp--Butter, softened
Pinch of--Salt

Directions
Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium heat.
Butter one side of each bread slice. Place one slice, buttered side down, on the skillet.
Add cheese slices on top of the bread in the skillet. Place the second slice of bread on top, buttered side up.
Grill until the bread is golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes. Flip the sandwich and grill the other side until golden brown and the cheese has melted.
Remove from heat, cut diagonally, and serve immediately.
Enjoy a delicious, simple, and classic grilled cheese sandwich
