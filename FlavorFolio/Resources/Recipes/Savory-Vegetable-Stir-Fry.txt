Title
Savory-Vegetable-Stir-Fry
Introduction

Ingredients
1cup--Bell peppers, mixed colors, sliced
1cup--Broccoli florets
1/2cup--Carrots, julienned
1/2cup--Snow peas
2tbsp--Soy sauce
1tbsp--Oyster sauce (optional)
2cloves--Garlic, minced
1tsp--Ginger, grated
2tbsp--Vegetable oil
1tsp--Sesame seeds, for garnish

Directions
Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large frying pan over high heat.
Add the garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds, until fragrant.
Add the bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snow peas. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce and oyster sauce. Pour this mixture over the vegetables. Stir well to coat the vegetables in the sauce.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Stir-fry for another minute, then remove from heat.
Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
Enjoy your vibrant and flavorful vegetable stir-fry!
