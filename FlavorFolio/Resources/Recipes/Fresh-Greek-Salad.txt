Title
Fresh-Greek-Salad
Introduction
Ingredients
2cups--Cherry tomatoes
1cup--Cucumber
1/2cup--Red onion
1/2cup--Kalamata olives
1cup--Feta cheese
2tbsp--Olive oil
1tbsp--Red wine vinegar
1tsp--Dried oregano

Directions
In a large bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, red onion, and Kalamata olives.
Add crumbled feta cheese to the bowl.
In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
Pour the dressing over the salad and gently toss to combine.
Let the salad sit for about 10 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
Serve chilled or at room temperature.
