Title
Fluffy-Cheese-and-Spinach-Omelette
Introduction
Indulge in a burst of flavors with our Fluffy Cheese and Spinach Omelette, a delightful breakfast or brunch option that combines the rich creaminess of cheddar cheese with the freshness of chopped spinach. This quick and easy recipe promises a fluffy and satisfying omelette experience that is sure to kickstart your day on a delicious note. Let's dive into creating this savory masterpiece that brings together the wholesome goodness of eggs, vibrant spinach, and the irresistible allure of melted cheese.

Ingredients
3--Eggs
1/2cup--Fresh spinach, chopped
1/4cup--Cheddar cheese, shredded
2tbsp--Milk
1tbsp--Butter

Directions
In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
Pour the egg mixture into the skillet. Let it cook without stirring for a minute until the bottom starts to set.
Sprinkle chopped spinach and shredded cheese over one half of the omelette.
Gently fold the other half over the filling. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the cheese melts and the omelette is cooked to your liking.
Carefully slide the omelette onto a plate.
Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
Enjoy your delicious and fluffy cheese and spinach omelette
